Pizza

A tasty pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Spread the dough onto a pan
  2. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough
  3. Spread the cheese and place the pepperoni on the sauce
  4. Set the oven to the correct temperature
  5. Place pan inside oven
  6. Let cook until ready
  7. Remove pan and turn off oven
  8. Let cool and cut into slices
  9. Enjoy
